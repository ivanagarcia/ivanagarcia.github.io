'Auckland: City of Sunlight' (2017)

Studying abroad in Vermont, USA, I wanted to create a video that put all the skills I learned about 3D camera, effects and Photoshop tools into action and put it into a video that showcased the place I grew up in: Auckland.

The audio was taken from a 1963 travel video, and the collage images are taken from all over the web.
