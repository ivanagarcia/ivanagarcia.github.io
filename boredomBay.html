Boredom Bay (2016)

Taking an idea from my neighbourhood and expanding it in an exaggerated way allowed me to explore moods, emotions, and feelings that are provoked when a visual is presented in a certain way, and made in a certain way. Tools and media used include: paper, gouache, acrylic, lino and more.

Special thanks to my friends, Lucy and Sandee who have allowed me to follow them around :-).
