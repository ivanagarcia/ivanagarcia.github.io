‘Kimi Kimi’ Cosmetics

The brand itself is called “Kimi Kimi” and features handmade typography amongst other handmade designs and illustrations using native New Zealand plants as brushes.

The word “Kimi” is a Maori word, meaning to seek, or to find, which I linked to the pursuit to perfection. Women are conditioned to always better themselves, physically, to be deemed worthy.
